Course Description

On site delivery supported by D2L courseware. Access to course materials on D2L and research materials on the Internet requires students to have regular access to the Internet.

Organization of Knowledge provides an introduction to information retrieval via the Web, metadata and managing information retrieval systems, and principles relating to the hybrid print-based and electronic networked environments.
