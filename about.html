About Me

This is what I do.

I'm basically on a mission to take the fear out of the first plunge into developing.

I started this blog because I always had so many questions. I was always confused about exactly how to start my career in Computer Science. I saw all the languages, and I often asked, "Which am I supposed to do first? Is there an order to these things?" I'm a person who desperately needs a plan at all times. Before I start anything, I have to make a checklist. Yet, my checklist for programming always seemed like a goals list, and that's not what I wanted. I knew where I was at and where I wanted to be, but everything in between was a mystery shrouded within in the vastness of the web.

I tried reading other blogs, which are full of helpful tidbits, but none of them dared to answer how you tackle the field. Unfortunately, I don't have any quirky grandma/grandpa stories to fill your time with either. I just want to get straight to the facts and try to be as blunt about my trials and errors as possible while outlining my experience with enough detail to assure anyone that they too can step off of the beaten path into the tech career they truly want.

Carpe diem.
